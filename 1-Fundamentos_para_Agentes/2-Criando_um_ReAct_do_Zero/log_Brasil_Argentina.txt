**Argentina – Quick Facts**

- **Capital:** Buenos Aires  
- **Population:** about 45.2 million people  
- **Official Language:** Spanish  
- **Currency:** Argentine peso (ARS)  
- **Time zone:** UTC‑03:00 (same as most of Brazil’s mainland)  
- **Area:** 2,780,400 km²  

Argentina is the second‑largest country in South America, known for its diverse landscapes—from the Andes mountains and Patagonia’s glaciers to the fertile Pampas plains. Its economy relies on agriculture (especially soybeans and beef), mining, and a growing services sector. The country shares a long border with Brazil to the northeast, and both nations operate on the same UTC‑03:00 time zone, making coordination across the border straightforward.
